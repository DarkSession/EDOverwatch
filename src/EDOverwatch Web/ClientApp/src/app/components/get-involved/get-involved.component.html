Get involved
If you like to help humanity in their efforts to stop this massive invasion you have a number of options depending on your playstyle.

Coordination: Defense Council of Humanity (DCoH)
The purpose of the Defense Council of Humanity (DCoH) is to address the defence of the bubble as a unified front and to work together to better deploy our individual factions to aid one another in this dangerous time. Only by working together will assure us of our victory.
Join the DCoH on Discord

Combat: Anti-Xeno Initiative (AXI)
The Anti-Xeno Initiative is an Elite:Dangerous player-group dedicated to protecting humanity from the Thargoid threat. We defend systems targeted by the vile xenos, study their technology and behavior, and perfect our battle tactics. Whether you seek to save the galaxy, kill some aliens, conduct research, or hone your skills, you will find help here.
Join the AXI on Discord

Logistics: Operation Ida (IDA)
Operation Ida is a non-partisan group dedicated to repairing damaged stations.
Join IDA on Discord

Rescue: Post Disaster Evacuation Service (PDES)
The Post Disaster Evacuation Service is a squadron dedicated to rescues from burning stations whenever there are some. Rescues provide a non combative way of helping to defeat Thargoid attacks for those who don't wish to fight the aliens, or to provide a mixture of activities for everyone else. Supplies and support are just as important as soldiers in a war.
Join the PDES on Discord

Operation: ReForge Aegis
A multi-faction coalition dedicated to Xeno Research and Defense, advocating for a new Aegis to lead a united humanity. Ranging from explorers, haulers, researchers, a few mad scientists, and some hardened ax pilots. We seek truth in the galaxy, while working to be the funnest group of CMDRs with hearts of gold. We work to maintain a respectful, welcoming, and engaging place for all to participate and enjoy the galaxy together.
Join the ReForge Aegis on Discord
