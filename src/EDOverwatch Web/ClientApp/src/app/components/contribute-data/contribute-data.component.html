Contribute
In order to give the community better insights about the efforts and the progress of the Thargoid war we need to collect Thargoid war related game journal from as many players as possible.

Sharing is caring
We make collected data available through our APIs so other great community projects can use it.
If you would like to contribute data you have the following options:

Running Elite: Dangerous Market Connector (or similar) PC only
Running the Elite: Dangerous Market Connector or any similar tool which transmits data to the EDDN while playing the game helps Overwatch and other community projects to collect data around systems, starports and more.

Overwatch Market Connector plug-in PC only
This is not available yet but eventually will be.
If you are running the Elite: Dangerous Market Connector and also want to contribute your commander data around the Thargoid war such as kills, rescues and supplies you can install this plug-in.

Game journal via Frontier servers
We give you the possibility to share your game journals from Frontier servers directly.
Your game journal is downloaded periodically and automatically from Frontier servers and processed in Overwatch.
We use this data to update systems and stations as well as to collect your contributions to the war efforts such as Thargoid kills, rescues and supplies.

We are following a privacy by design in Overwatch which is why beside the Frontier customer Id and commander name we do not collect any data related to users.

You can authorize us to access your game journals by using the button below. Once authorized, we usually have access to your game journals for about 28 days. After this time expires, you need to grant authorization again if you wish to continue to contribute your data this way.
You can revoke the authorization at any time in the Frontier user portal under Authorised applications.
