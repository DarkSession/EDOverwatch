Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Where does the data come from?
Overwatch automatically collects and processes data from different sources:
EDDN - Elite Dangerous Data Network : Information about stations, systems and their states.

INARA: Information about player contributions such as kills and rescues.

Operation IDA spreadsheet: Information about deliveres and missions.

Overwatch (player journal import): Information about player contributions such as kills and rescues as well as stations, systems and their states.

DCoH Discord: Information in screenshots shared by players are extracted and update the system progress and remaining time.

Why does the number of systems not match the number in-game?
After the weekly tick on Thursday 7am UTC it usually takes a few hours until Overwatch received and processed all data about new alerts and invasions.
However, after that the difference is likely systems under invisible Thargoid control.
